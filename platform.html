The SRPVDAL Engine

A deterministic, 7-stage pipeline ensuring that every autonomous action is reasoned, planned, and verified before execution.

Stage 1: Sense

Ingests live signals from ERP, CRM, and cloud stacks in real-time.

Stage 2: Reason

Applies causal modeling and knowledge graphs to evaluate outcomes.

Stage 3: Plan

Creates step-by-step reasoning chains for complex operational problems.

Stage 4: Verify

Adversarial "Devil's Advocate" review catches blind spots before action.

Stage 5: Decide

Credibility-weighted multi-agent voting ensures safe consensus.

Stage 6: Act

Executes decisions automatically with pre-state capture for instant rollback.

Stage 7: Learn

Updates models continuously based on real-world outcomes.
