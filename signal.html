CAPABILITIES

From attribution guesswork to governed acquisition.

Signal does not report last-click metrics or optimize in a silo. It traces what actually moves customers, then steers spend and creative through the same SRDAL loop and Decision Control Plane that govern every other enterprise action.

Causal Attribution

Signal doesn't guess which touchpoint drove a conversion. It traces the causal chain — what actually influenced the outcome, what was incidental, and what would have happened if the touchpoint hadn't existed. Attribution built on counterfactual evidence, not last-click correlation.

Autonomous Bid Optimization

Media buying decisions flow through the SRDAL loop and the Decision Control Plane. Every bid adjustment is causally justified, policy-compliant, and scored against simulated alternatives before execution. No black-box automation — governed autonomy.

Threshold-Aware Execution

Signal understands the activation thresholds and learning stability dynamics of advertising platforms. It doesn't fight the algorithm — it works with the platform's own optimization mechanics while maintaining causal oversight.
