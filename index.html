The Paradigm

Enterprise AI has learned to reason. It has not yet learned to be accountable.

Most AI systems stop at recommendation. They summarize, draft, and suggest — then hand the decision back to a human. MIZOKI3 closes the loop. It decides. It acts. It governs every action through a patented control plane. And it proves, with full causal evidence, why it did what it did.

The Platform

Three layers. One governed intelligence.

Temporal-Causal Knowledge Graph

A living map of cause and effect.

MIZOKI3 maintains a continuously evolving graph of entities, events, contracts, and outcomes — tracking not just what happened, but what caused it and what would happen if conditions changed. Every decision makes the system smarter.

SRPVDAL Decision Engine

Seven stages. Zero shortcuts.

Every decision flows through Sense, Reason, Plan, Validate, Decide, Act, and Learn. Each stage is auditable. Each handoff is verified. The pipeline ensures that no action is taken without causal justification and policy compliance.

Decision Control Plane

AI proposes. The Gate authorizes.

The DCP is a mandatory, non-bypass governance layer between what AI agents recommend and what actually executes. Every action is scored on causal confidence, compliance, simulation outcomes, and historical performance before it touches your systems. Patented.
