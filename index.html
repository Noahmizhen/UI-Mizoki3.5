Decision Control Plane (DCP)

Separates Proposal from Authorization

Propose

Agents generate options and recommendations.

Verify

Independent verification by Risk, Policy, and Compliance agents.

Authorize

Human or policy-based authorization at the right threshold.

Execute

Action is executed, monitored, and logged.
