CAPABILITIES

From spreadsheet snapshots to causal foresight.

Capital does not refresh dashboards on a schedule. It reads covenants, treasury position, and counterparty behavior as living causal nodes — projecting breach probability and liquidity stress before they become crises.

Covenant Breach Forecasting

Capital monitors every financial covenant across your credit agreements, tracking the causal factors that drive compliance metrics. When conditions shift — revenue trends, cash flow patterns, market movements — Capital projects the breach probability and timeline before it becomes a crisis.

Liquidity Stress Modeling

Counterfactual simulation applied to treasury management. Capital models what happens to your cash position under alternative scenarios — a client defaulting, a revenue line declining, a credit facility tightening — and surfaces the causal chain behind each outcome.

Capital Allocation Optimization

Every allocation recommendation flows through the Decision Control Plane. Capital doesn't just suggest where to deploy resources — it justifies the recommendation with causal evidence, simulates the alternatives, and verifies compliance with every covenant and policy constraint before authorization.

CROSS-DOMAIN INTELLIGENCE

When Counsel maps a new covenant restriction, Capital inherits it as a hard constraint instantly — no manual handoff, no email chain, no spreadsheet update. When Signal detects a shift in customer acquisition costs, Capital factors it into cash flow projections automatically. The knowledge graph makes financial intelligence continuous, not periodic.
